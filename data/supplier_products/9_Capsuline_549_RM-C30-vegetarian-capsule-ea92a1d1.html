ingredients

Recommended Storage

◉ Store the empty vegetarian capsules in a cool dry place to keep them from getting cracked, deformed, or swell if exposed to humidity.

◉ The optimal temperature for empty capsules is 59°-86 °FH / 15°-30°C with a relative humidity between 35-70%.

◉ If properly stored, these capsules can last up to 5 years!
