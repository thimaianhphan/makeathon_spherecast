Custom Probiotic Formulations Tailored to Your Needs

Trusted for Over 20 Years

Custom Probiotics, Inc. formulates and supplies high-potency, professional-grade custom probiotic supplements for both children and adults. Our single-strain and multi-strain formulations are designed to support sensitive and specialized dietary needs while maintaining the highest standards of purity and quality.

Probiotics are beneficial bacteria that support a balanced intestinal microbiota and overall gut health. Our approach focuses on delivering pure probiotic strains without unnecessary ingredients, allowing for targeted and precise supplementation.

Pure Probiotic Supplements with No Additives

All Custom Probiotics supplements are allergen-free and formulated without fillers, carriers, or artificial ingredients. Our products contain no sugar, gluten, soy, casein, yeast, maltodextrin, artificial colors, flavors, preservatives, FOS, inulin, or genetically modified ingredients.

Capsule and Powder Probiotic Options

Custom Probiotics offers supplements in both capsule and high-quality powder forms , providing flexible options for children and adults. This allows individuals and healthcare professionals to choose formats that align with specific dietary and dosing preferences.

High-Potency, Professional-Grade Probiotics

Our probiotic supplements range from 60 billion CFUs per capsule to 100–400 billion CFUs per gram in powder form, measured at expiration. Each formulation is produced with careful attention to potency, stability, and consistency.

Probiotics are defined as non-pathogenic live microorganisms that, when administered in adequate amounts, contribute to overall nutritional balance and digestive health.
